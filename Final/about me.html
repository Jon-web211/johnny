About Us

Who am I?

My name is Jonathan, I am 19 years old and I live in a rural city called Pueblo.

I have been working as an amateur photographer for several years at this point. I focus more on portrait, landscape, sports, architecture and abstract photography. I will do everything in my power to make your vision into a reality.

Always setting myself more goals is essential for me to continue to move forward and to be able to progress and surpass myself.
